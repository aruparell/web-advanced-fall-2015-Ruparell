Aarti Ruparell

A native Canadian, Aarti was born and raised in Calgary, Alberta, moving to Toronto at age 12. She completed her undergraduate degree (BBA) in International Business, and then moved to New York City to pursue her Fashion Marketing degree at Parsons The New School for Design. Now residing in Manhattan she works in various feilds including as professional MakeUp Artist (fashion editorial & weddings) Ecommerce, Digital Marketing as well as a 300RYT hour Yoga Teacher. She continues to work for industry leading companies including Michael Kors, Solstice Sunglasses, L'Oreal & Equinox. Aarti also completed her MSc in Media Management at The New School of Public Engagement, and is a 2017 candidate for MFA in Design & Technology at Parsons, The New School for Design.
